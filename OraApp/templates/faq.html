  • What is DEIR?

    DEIR stands for Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, and Retention. It is a recruitment-tech platform that supports ethical and inclusive hiring practices in STEM. DEIR offers unique resources such as anonymous recruitment, job benchmarking, ethical job posting, a standardized interview process, mentoring retention program, and an endorsement badge for ethical and inclusive employers.

  • Who can use DEIR?

    DEIR is open to all employers and job seekers in the STEM field. We encourage all employers who are committed to promoting diversity and inclusion in their hiring practices to join our platform. Women and other underrepresented groups in STEM are particularly encouraged to use DEIR to find job opportunities in a safe and inclusive environment.

  • How does DEIR support diversity and inclusion in hiring practices?

    DEIR supports diversity and inclusion in hiring practices through its unique resources such as anonymous recruitment, job benchmarking, ethical job posting, standardized interview process, mentoring retention program, and an endorsement badge for ethical and inclusive employers. These resources help eliminate bias in the recruitment process and create a more inclusive and equitable hiring environment.

  • How does anonymous recruitment work on DEIR?

    Anonymous recruitment on DEIR allows job seekers to submit their applications without revealing personal information such as their name, gender, or ethnicity. This helps eliminate unconscious bias in the recruitment process and ensures that candidates are evaluated based on their qualifications and experience rather than personal characteristics.

  • How does job benchmarking work on DEIR?

    Job benchmarking on DEIR involves creating a standardized job description that includes the key skills, competencies, and experience required for a particular position. This helps employers evaluate candidates objectively and ensure that all candidates are evaluated based on the same criteria.

  • How does the mentoring retention program on DEIR work?

    The mentoring retention program on DEIR matches job seekers with experienced mentors in their field who can provide guidance and support throughout their job search and career development. This program helps job seekers build their networks and gain valuable insights into the industry, while also supporting their long-term career growth and retention.

  • How does the endorsement badge for ethical and inclusive employers work on DEIR?

    The endorsement badge on DEIR is awarded to employers who demonstrate a commitment to ethical and inclusive hiring practices. Employers can earn the badge by completing a self-assessment and meeting certain criteria, such as using anonymous recruitment, offering mentorship programs, and promoting diversity and inclusion in their workplace.   • Is DEIR free to use?

    DEIR offers Monthly and Yearly paid subscription plans for employers. Job seekers can use DEIR for free to search for job opportunities and create a profile.

  • How can I get support if I have questions or issues with DEIR?

    DEIR offers customer support through a variety of channels, including email, phone, and chat. You can email us directly at inquiry@istemco.com to get in touch.
